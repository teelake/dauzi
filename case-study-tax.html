Tax Dispute Resolution

Resolved multi-year tax assessments through evidence-based engagement, improved documentation, and negotiated settlement strategies.

Challenge

A multinational manufacturing company was facing multiple tax assessments spanning five years, totaling over $2.5M. The assessments covered corporate income tax, withholding tax, and VAT, with the tax authority asserting aggressive positions on transfer pricing, permanent establishment, and expense deductibility.

Approach

We conducted a comprehensive review and developed a structured response:

  • Reconstructed transaction documentation and prepared technical position papers
  • Identified valid grounds for objection and quantified potential liabilities under different scenarios
  • Engaged with the tax authority through formal objections and structured negotiations
  • Supported the client's in-house team with strategy and representation during appeals

Outcomes

  • 75% reduction in total tax liability through negotiated settlement
  • Closure of all five years within 18 months of engagement
  • Improved compliance framework: Established processes to prevent future disputes
  • Cash flow preservation: Avoided immediate payment of disputed amounts, allowing operations to continue

Client: Multinational Manufacturing Company

Duration: 18 months

Scope: Tax Dispute Resolution, Transfer Pricing, Corporate Tax
